Projects

GIS–FA for prediction in untested environments

End-to-end R pipeline integrating factor–analytic mixed models, environmental data (climate, soil, spectral) and thematic maps to recommend common bean hybrids in regions without experimental trials.

  • R · asreml · GISFA · PLS

G×E dashboard with factor-analytic models

Shiny application for exploring factor loadings, scores, recommendation maps, and hybrid stability under FA models in multi-environment trials.

  • R · shiny · qgraph
  • Interactive visualisation

Genomic prediction integrated with enviromics

Scripts and tutorials for reaction-norm models combining SNP markers and environmental covariates to predict complex traits across environments.

  • Genomics · GBLUP · Reaction norm
  • Quarto vignettes

About me

  • Biotechnological Engineer | PhD Candidate in Genetics and Plant Breeding at UFV.
  • Background in plant tissue culture, with expertise in protoplast technology, mutagenesis, suspension cell culture, and elicitation strategies.
  • Research focused on Genotype-by-Environment Interaction, genomics and enviromics, integrating environmental covariates to enhance breeding strategies.
  • Skilled in quantitative genetics, statistical modelling, and experimental design to support data-driven breeding decisions.
  • Experience in data analysis, predictive modelling, and computational tools (R).
  • Committed to bridging genetics, statistics and biometrics to develop high-performing crops, leveraging big data and machine-learning approaches to accelerate genetic gain in plant breeding.
